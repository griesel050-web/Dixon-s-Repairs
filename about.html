Who We Are

Precision Runs in Our DNA

Dixon's Starter and Alternator Repairs is a specialist auto electrical workshop based in Booysens, Pretoria. We focus exclusively on the starting and charging systems of vehicles — and that focused expertise is exactly what sets us apart.

We work on everything from everyday passenger cars to heavy commercial trucks and equipment. Whether your bakkie won't crank on a cold morning or your alternator warning light has come on, our team has the knowledge and tools to diagnose and fix the problem correctly the first time.

Our approach is simple: honest advice, quality workmanship, fair pricing. We'll tell you exactly what's wrong, what it'll cost, and what your options are. No unnecessary parts, no runaround.
