What We Offer

Our Services

Specialist auto electrical services covering everything from quick diagnostics to full rebuilds — for all vehicle types.

⚡ 01

Starter Motor Repair

A faulty starter motor leaves you stranded. We fully disassemble your unit, inspect and replace brushes, bushings and the solenoid, test the armature and commutator, then rebuild and bench-test to factory specifications before returning it to you.

  • Full disassembly & inspection
  • Brush & bushing replacement
  • Armature and commutator testing
  • Solenoid check & replacement
  • Bench testing before return
🔋 02

Alternator Repair & Recon

Your alternator keeps the battery charged and powers all electrical systems while driving. We test charging output, inspect stator and rotor windings, replace diode packs and voltage regulators, and restore your alternator to proper charging performance.

  • Charging output testing
  • Stator & rotor inspection
  • Diode pack replacement
  • Voltage regulator service
  • Full reconditioning available
🔍 03

Electrical Diagnostics

Not sure what's wrong? We use professional diagnostic equipment to test your starting and charging circuits in full. We'll pinpoint the exact fault — saving you from guessing and replacing parts that don't need replacing.

  • Starting circuit load testing
  • Charging system analysis
  • Battery condition testing
  • Circuit continuity checks
  • Clear written fault report
🔄 04

Component Exchange

Need to be back on the road the same day? We maintain a range of tested, reconditioned exchange units for popular vehicle models. Hand in your old unit and drive away with a fully tested replacement — fast and cost-effective.

  • Same-day turnaround
  • All exchange units fully tested
  • Popular SA vehicle models stocked
  • Cost-effective alternative to new
🚛 05

Heavy Commercial Units

Trucks, buses, agricultural and construction equipment place extreme demands on their electrical systems. Our workshop handles high-torque starters and high-amperage alternators from all major heavy commercial manufacturers.

  • Trucks & buses
  • Agricultural equipment
  • Construction machinery
  • High-torque starter motors
  • High-amperage alternators (24V)
🛠️ 06

Parts & Accessories

We stock a wide range of auto electrical components and can source specialist parts for older and imported vehicles. If you know what you need, come to us before heading to the dealer — we're often faster and more affordable.

  • Brushes & brush holders
  • Slip rings & commutators
  • Solenoids & relays
  • Bushings & bearings
  • Voltage regulators & diode packs
