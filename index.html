What We Do

Our Services

⚡
Starter Motor Repair

Full disassembly, brush replacement, armature testing, and rebuild of starter motors. We restore your starter to factory-spec performance or advise on best-value replacement options.

🔋
Alternator Repair & Recon

Comprehensive alternator testing, stator and rotor inspection, diode pack replacement, and voltage regulator service. We ensure your charging system keeps your battery topped up.

🔍
Electrical Diagnostics

Using professional diagnostic equipment, we pinpoint faults in your vehicle's starting and charging circuits quickly — saving you time and unnecessary part replacements.

🔄
Component Exchange

Need a quick solution? We offer tested, reconditioned exchange units for popular models — so you're back on the road without the wait of a full rebuild.

🚛
Heavy Commercial Units

We service starters and alternators from light vehicles through to trucks and heavy equipment. Our workshop is equipped to handle high-torque and high-amperage units.

🛠️
Parts & Accessories

Brushes, slip rings, solenoids, bushings and more — we stock a wide range of auto electrical components and can source specialist parts for older or imported vehicles.
